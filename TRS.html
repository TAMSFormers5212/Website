TAMS Repair Society

TAMS Repair Society is a committee that repairs many objects such as devices, vehicles, and instruments for other TAMS students.
We also host workshops for TAMS students who want to learn about different components of technology.
We're also planning to volunteer at local middle schools and give presentations about technology.
