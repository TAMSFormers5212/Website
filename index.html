About Us

We are FRC 5212, otherwise known as TAMSformers Robotics! Last year, we competed at the Texas district FRC competitions at Dallas and Amarillo. Our award winning program even reached

  • FRC Worlds in 2014, 2015, and 2018
  • 3rd Place at the 2023 Amarillo District Tournament
  • Much more

All of our team members are students of the Texas Academy of Math and Science, an early college STEM program at UNT.
